# eBay Content Creation

## Overview

A complex internal tool used by eBay marketing teams to create and manage content across multiple channels. Built to handle large-scale, collaborative workflows with a highly dynamic frontend.

## Role & Contributions

Led development of new features from concept to production, improving usability and efficiency for marketing teams.

Maintained and refactored existing code in a large, multi-team codebase, ensuring stability and maintainability.

Collaborated across teams to integrate functionality smoothly into the broader platform.

Migrated legacy components from Backbone.js to React, improving maintainability and developer velocity.

## Impact

Streamlined the content creation process, reducing friction for teams working on complex campaigns.

Improved system performance and developer feedback loops through optimizations and CI improvements.

Laid the groundwork for further automation and AI integration within the content pipeline.
