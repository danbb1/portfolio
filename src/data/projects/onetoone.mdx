# One to One Tutoring

## Web Design and Development

[https://www.bournemouthtutor.co.uk/](https://www.bournemouthtutor.co.uk/)

One to One Tutoring is an expansion of a long-established private tutoring business into the online world. The brief was to create a website that was bespoke and personal to attract new students.

It is built with Gatsby and Tailwind.

Formik with Yup validation handles form management and Netlify's built in form submissions handles submissions, filtering out spam with a bot honeypot.
